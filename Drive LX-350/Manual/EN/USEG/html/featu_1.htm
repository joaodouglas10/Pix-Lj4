Features

Your EPSON® LX-350 9-pin dot matrix printer provides high-quality printing and superior performance in a compact design. Its features include:
The ability to handle a wide range of paper types, including continuous paper, multipart forms (with one original and up to four copies), single sheets, envelopes, and roll paper.
High-speed printing of up to 347 cps (characters per second) at 10 cpi (characters per inch).
Your printer comes packaged with the printer driver and other software, including the EPSON Status Monitor 3 utility for Microsoft Windows 7, Vista, XP, and 2000. The EPSON Status Monitor 3 utility lets you check the status of your printer quickly and easily.
